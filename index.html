Frequently Asked Questions

Can I sell items now and deliver them later? ▶
Yes, you can list your item now and coordinate delivery timing directly with the buyer.
I'm a fresher — can I use XChangeIt? ▶
Yes, all verified GEC Palakkad students can use XChangeIt to buy and sell items.
How do I contact a seller? ▶
Use the "Chat with Seller" button available on every product page.
Is it free to list items? ▶
Yes, listing items on XChangeIt is completely free for students.
Does XChangeIt handle payments? ▶
Payments are handled directly between buyer and seller by mutual agreement.
